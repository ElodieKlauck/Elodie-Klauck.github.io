Imposer sa personnalité, de la tête aux pieds

Partagez notre passion et vivez en harmonie avec vous-mêmes. Chez Sockies, on aime la folie, l'humour, la timidité et l'envie. On choisit nos chaussettes, celles qui déterminerons notre tenue, et celles qui nous emmèneront au bout du monde. On choisit les chaussettes qui nous correspondent, mais aussi celles que l'on n'ose pas. Sockies propose, et vous éblouissez.

Nous pensons aussi aux petits petons

Parce que nous voyons les choses en grand, nous avons aussi prévu de penser dans les moindres détails. Cette collection est dédiée aux plus petits pieds de votre famille, ceux qui commencent à gambader, et ceux qui courent vers vous.

Our values

Our ecological impact

Sockies has implemented eco-friendly practices in its production process, including using recycled materials, reducing water waste, and using energy-efficient systems. The brand has also adopted sustainable packaging and has a program for disposing of waste. These efforts have earned recognition from environmental organizations and are hoped to set a positive example in the industry.

Our involvement with people

We attach importance to community and solidarity, by promoting a sense of belonging and unity among employees, customers, and partners. We prioritize ethical and sustainable practices and support local communities to achieve a better world for everyone.

The importance of creativity

Creativity is at the heart of everything we do at Sockies. Our goal is to bring a new level of fun and excitement to the world of socks, and we believe that this can only be achieved through a relentless pursuit of creativity. This is why we allow customers to give us ideas and feedbacks to developp the brand's creativity.
